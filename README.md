# Civio Graphs Public

📊 Open-source data visualizations by [Civio](https://civio.es/).

## Directory

| Chart | Description | Languages | Stack | Published |
|-------|-------------|-----------|-------|-----------|
| [VPO Loss Breakdown by Region](lopublico/perdida-vpo/despiece-ccaa) | Per-region breakdown of Spain's social housing (VPO) since 1991. Pairs a stacked-area chart of protection states with a Gantt-style timeline of regulations; the reader's region is auto-detected by IP and prev/next nav crossfades between the 19 autonomous communities. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | May 2026 |
| [VPO Protection Periods 2026](lopublico/perdida-vpo/plazos-vpo-2026) | Color-coded grid comparing the protection periods of publicly subsidised housing (VPO) in 2026 across Spain's autonomous communities. Each region's band expands on click to reveal the period set per housing type. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | May 2026 |
| [Cumulative VPO Loss](lopublico/perdida-vpo/scroll-desaparicion-vpo) | Scrollytelling visualization of Spain's protected housing stock (1991–2030, projected from 2026). Six narrative steps walk through built stock, permanent vs. temporary protection, loss and voluntary disqualification; the final step lets the reader explore the data across protection, plan, developer and tenure dimensions. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | May 2026 |
| [Isolation Sanction Reasons](justicia/aislamiento-prisiones/motivos-sanciones) | Interactive square-packing visualization showing prison isolation sanctions by disciplinary reason code (2020-2025). Concentric year rings per motivo, filterable by administration and gender. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | March 2026 |
| [Sanctions by Prison Administration](justicia/aislamiento-prisiones/sanciones-administracion) | Stacked bar chart comparing solitary confinement sanctions and protective isolation across Spain's three prison administrations (AGE, Catalonia, Basque Country) over time. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | March 2026 |
| [Time in Limbo](justicia/aislamiento-prisiones/tiempos-limbo) | Canvas-based waffle chart visualizing how long inmates spend in isolation in Spanish prisons. Shows 7,800 cases distributed across 5 duration groups, with the UN-recommended 15-day maximum highlighted. | Spanish | Svelte 5, Vite 7, D3 | March 2026 |
